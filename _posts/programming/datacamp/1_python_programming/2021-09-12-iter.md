### Iterable
* 반복 가능한 것(string, list, tuple, dictionary, set, range 등)

### Iterator
* ```iter``` 함수를 통해 생성된 객체

---


```python
flash = ['jay garrick', 'barry allen', 'wally west', 'bart allen']

superhero = iter(flash)
print(next(superhero))
print(next(superhero))
print(next(superhero))
print(next(superhero))

# 위와 같음
# for person in flash:
#     print(person)
```
